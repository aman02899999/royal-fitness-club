FAQ

Do I need to be an RFC gym member to use the app? ▼
The basic features (Beast Score, Workout Logger, Streak) are free for everyone. The PRO features (Blood Analyser, Meal Plans, 108 Guides, 10 Courses, Leaderboard prize) require an active RFC gym membership of 3+ months to unlock for free, or a ₹499/month subscription for non-members.
How do I get my gym membership code? ▼
Ask at the Royal Fitness Club front desk — they'll give you your personal membership code. You enter it in the app after signing in with Google. The system automatically verifies your membership duration.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. All data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) with secure authentication. Blood report values are entered manually by you and never shared. Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties.
Can I use it on my phone without installing anything? ▼
Yes — the app is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Open it in Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone, and it works like a native app. You can add it to your home screen for instant access without visiting an app store.
How is the Tiger Leaderboard prize distributed? ▼
The top 3 ranked members at the end of each month win a supplement stack (₹15,000 value for #1, ₹5,000 for #2, ₹2,500 for #3). Winners are announced at the gym and contacted directly. The leaderboard resets on the 1st of each month.
