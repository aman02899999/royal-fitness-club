🫀 Foam Rolling Protocol

Roll each area for 30–60 sec. Pause on tight spots. Breathe through it.

🦵
Quads / IT Band
Lie face down. Roll from hip to knee. Pause on knots 10–20 sec. Rotate leg in/out.
⏱ 60 sec each side
🍑
Glutes / Piriformis
Sit on roller with one ankle over opposite knee. Lean into glute. Small circles.
⏱ 45 sec each side
🔙
Upper Back (Thoracic)
Roller across mid-back. Arms crossed or hands behind head. Extend back over roller.
⏱ 60 sec total
🦶
Calves
Sit with roller under calf. Lift hips. Roll from ankle to knee. Flex/point foot.
⏱ 45 sec each side
💪
Lats / Thoracic
Side-lying with arm extended overhead. Roll from armpit down to mid-back.
⏱ 45 sec each side
🦴
Hip Flexors
Face down, roller at front of hip. Rock slowly side to side. Lift knee to intensify.
⏱ 45 sec each side
Pro tip: Roll before training to warm up and increase range. Roll after training for recovery. Never roll directly on a joint or the lower spine.
