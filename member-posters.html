ROYAL FITNESS CLUB

Rules every Royal member lives by
01
RE-RACK YOUR WEIGHTS
Always return dumbbells, plates and barbells to their designated place after every set. Respect the space you share.
02
WIPE DOWN EQUIPMENT
Carry a towel. Clean every machine and bench before and after use. Hygiene is a sign of respect for fellow members.
03
NO PHONE HOGGING
Limit rest periods to 2–3 minutes during peak hours. Step off equipment while scrolling. Others are waiting.
04
RESPECT THE COMMUNITY
No unsolicited advice. No ego lifting. No intimidation. Every member is on their own journey. Encourage, don't discourage.
05
DRESS CODE & HYGIENE
Wear proper gym attire. Closed-toe shoes are mandatory on the gym floor. Deodorant is not optional.
06
STAY HYDRATED, STAY SAFE
Drink water regularly. Use a spotter for heavy lifts. Inform staff if you feel unwell. Your safety is our priority.
07
MEMBERSHIP IS PERSONAL
Your membership card is non-transferable. Entry is for registered members only. Guests must be registered at the front desk.

M8 — Member Nutrition Guide (700×600)
ROYAL FITNESS CLUB
EAT LIKE
AN ELITE
🥩
PROTEIN FIRST
Target 1.6–2.2g of protein per kg of bodyweight daily. Prioritise chicken, eggs, paneer, daal & whey. Every meal should have a protein source.
⏰
MEAL TIMING
Eat a protein-carb meal 90 minutes pre-workout. Post-workout: 30g protein + fast carbs within 45 minutes of finishing your session.
💧
HYDRATION RULE
Drink at least 35ml per kg bodyweight daily. Add 500ml for every hour of training. Dehydration kills strength and focus.
🍛
INDIAN DIET HACKS
Dal + rice = complete protein. Use ghee over refined oils. Add turmeric for recovery. Curd post-workout beats whey in absorption speed.
🚫
AVOID THESE
Maida, packaged juices, sugar-loaded protein bars, fried snacks before training. These spike insulin and tank your energy mid-session.
💊
SMART SUPPLEMENTS
Creatine 5g/day, Vitamin D3 + K2, Magnesium before bed, Omega-3 with food. These are the only 4 supplements every member actually needs.
