## Key Features

### 1. Beast Score — Body Composition Analysis
Proprietary RFC calculator. Enter your stats and get:
- BMI, BMR, TDEE (maintenance calories)
- Body fat % + lean mass estimation
- Macro targets (protein / carbs / fat)
- Overall fitness score out of 100
- Personalised training + nutrition recommendation

### 2. Smart Meal Planner (PRO)
Formula-based 7-day Indian meal plan generator. Inputs:
- Goal (fat loss / muscle gain / recomposition / maintenance)
- Dietary preference (veg / non-veg / vegan)
- 17 health conditions (diabetes, PCOS, thyroid, hypertension, etc.)
- Budget, activity level, calorie target

Output: Full 7-day plan with breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, macros per meal.

### 3. Blood Report AI Analyser (PRO)
Upload blood test PDF or enter values manually. RFC analyses:
- 40+ blood markers (Hb, WBC, glucose, HbA1c, lipids, liver enzymes, thyroid, hormones, vitamins)
- Smart modern fitness & nutrition recommendations per marker
- **Ayurvedic approach panel** — every abnormal marker links to the corresponding Ayurvedic condition with herbs, formulations, diet guidance, and yoga

### 4. Ayurveda Hub
37 health conditions covered with complete Ayurvedic protocols:
- Primary herbs + clinical doses
- Classical Ayurvedic formulations
- Dosha analysis (Vata / Pitta / Kapha)
- Diet do's and don'ts
- Lifestyle and yoga guidance
- Packs auto-populated from blood report or diet calculator

### 5. Diet Calculator with Ayurvedic Integration
Select health conditions and RFC generates:
- Personalised macro targets
- Recommended Indian foods
- Foods to avoid
- Inline Ayurvedic approach cards for selected conditions
- One-tap navigation to full Ayurvedic protocols

### 6. 12-Week Transformation Roadmap (PRO)
Personalised week-by-week plan covering:
- Training phases (adaptation → hypertrophy → peak)
- Nutrition targets per phase
- Recovery protocols
- Milestone check-ins

### 7. Workout Tracker & Exercise Library
- Log sets, reps, weight for any exercise
- 15+ specialty workout tabs with 50+ animated GIF exercises (beginner to advanced)
- Categories: HIIT, Cardio, Core, Strength, Mobility, Plyometrics, Recovery

### 8. Progress Tracker (PRO)
- Before/after photo uploads
- Body measurement logging (weight, waist, hips, arms, legs)
- Body fat % trend chart
- BF gauge visualization
- Photo upload with progress comparison

### 9. XP & Gamification
- XP points earned for every action (logging meals, workouts, streaks)
- 5 levels: Rookie → Warrior → Beast → Royal → Legend (XP thresholds)
- Weekly challenges with XP rewards
- Global leaderboard with city + goal filters
- Streak system with loss-aversion warnings (+ Streak Freeze for PRO)

### 10. Physiotherapy & Injury Rehab
Evidence-based rehab protocols for:
- Shoulder, knee, back, ankle injuries
- POLICE protocol (Protection, Optimal Loading, Ice, Compression, Elevation)
- Return-to-sport criteria checklists
- Prehabilitation exercise programs

### 11. Push Notifications
Smart notification system for:
- Workout reminders
- Streak protection alerts
- New content updates
- Challenge deadlines
- Custom scheduling

### 12. Admin Panel (Admin-only)
Full backend management:
- User management (approve/reject/upgrade/expire members)

- PDF guide management
- Membership plan editor
- Live theme editor (per-section color customization)
